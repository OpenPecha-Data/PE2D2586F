§20 athāyuṣmān ānandas trāṇamuktaṃ bodhisattvam evam avocat | kathaṃ kulaputra parikṣīṇāyuḥ
punar nivartate |trāṇamukto bodhisattva āha | na tvayā bhadantānanda tathāgatasyāntikāc chrutam
santi navākālamaraṇāni | teṣāṃ pratikṣepeṇa mantrauṣadhiprayogā upadiṣṭāḥ |santi sattvā
vyādhitā na ca guruko vyādhiḥ kiṃ tu bhaiṣajyopasthāyakavirahitaḥ | yadi vā vaidyā abhaiṣajyaṃ
kurvanti | te tato nidānaṃ kālaṃ kurvanti idaṃ prathamam akālamaraṇam |dvitīyam akālamaraṇaṃ
yasya rājadaṇḍena kālakriyā |tṛtīyam akālamaraṇaṃ ye 'tīva pramādavihāriṇas teṣām
amanuṣyā ojo 'paharanti |caturtham akālamaraṇaṃ ye agnidāhena kālaṃ kurvanti |pañcamam
akālamaraṇaṃ ye ca udake mriyanti |ṣaṣṭham akālamaraṇaṃ ye siṃhavyāghravyāḍacaṇḍamṛgamadhyagatā
maranti |saptamam akālamaraṇaṃ ye giritaṭāt prapatanti |aṣṭamam akālamaraṇaṃ ye viṣakākhordavetāḍānuprayogeṇa
maranti |navamam akālamaraṇaṃ ye kṣuttarṣopahatā āhārapānam alabhamānāḥ
kālaṃ kurvanti |imāni saṃkṣepeṇa navākālamaraṇāni tathāgateṇa nirdiṣṭāni | anyāni
cāprameyāṇy akālamaraṇāni ||
